About Skill Sync

Skill Sync is a live peer-to-peer learning platform. Two people join the same room — one shares what they know, the other learns it — using a shared whiteboard, video & slides, real-time chat, and a built-in learning assistant.

What makes it different

  • 🤝 Two-way knowledge sharing — anyone can teach or learn.
  • 🎓 Certification — pass a 10-question quiz (7/10) to become a certified teacher and earn a downloadable certificate.
  • 🖊️ Live tools — whiteboard, video, slides and chat that sync instantly between devices.
  • 🔒 Private by design — your data lives on your device and the room you choose; no external services.

Our mission

To make learning a skill as simple as sharing a room code — turning everyday knowledge into a teaching opportunity for everyone.
